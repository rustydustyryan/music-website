The Why

If you are like me then you're tired of giant corporations sucking the soul out of the music industry with their greedy antics - we're looking at you Spotify and Apple Music. I want to put my attention and money elswhere. That is why I have created this website, to give people a place where good music is celebrated!

Eventually I want to expand this idea and start paying the artists that I feature. But for now I will contribute with exposure and hopefully additional enthusiastic fans. I would love it if you treated this site like your Discover Weekly playlist instead of supporting the streaming tycoons.

Let me know if you have any artists or songs that I should be excited about, as well as ideas for growing this website and how to better support artists.


I would love to hear from you.

Thank you!
